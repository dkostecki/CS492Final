Bill Pay

Pay your major bills online from a single site with just one password. After enrolling in the service through Online Banking, you can even pay using the Mobile Banking app on your mobile device.Footnote1

Features:

  • Many bills can be paid electronically as soon as the next business day (utilities, cable, phone)Footnote2
  • Pay your Bank of America® credit card the same day it's dueFootnote3
  • Schedule one-time or recurring payments to help get peace of mind
  • Track and review up to 24 months of payment history
  • Set up alerts to help you stay on top of your transactionsFootnote4
opens a pop up video

Watch the video to see how Bill Pay works.

Getting started:

  1. 1. Sign in to Online Banking and click Bill Pay.
  2. 1. Sign in to Mobile Banking and click Bill Pay.
  3. 2. Add Pay To Accounts (the person or company you will be paying). After entering a Pay To Account once, it will be available in Online and Mobile Banking.
  4. 3. After setting up your Pay To Account(s), you can make payments by entering the Amount and Deliver By Date.
  5. 4. Select Make Payment at the bottom of the screen.
Remember: Your Online Banking transactions are protected with our Online and Mobile Banking Security Guarantee.Footnote5

Transfers

Sending money is fast, easy and secure when you use Transfers.

With Transfers, you can:

  • Transfer money to and from your Bank of America accounts or your accounts at other banksFootnote1
  • Set up automatic transfers directly from your checking to savings account to help make saving easier
  • Transfer money to pay back friends through Online Banking or the Mobile Banking appFootnote2 using their email address or mobile phone numberFootnote3
  • Pay your Bank of America credit card and mortgage accounts quickly and conveniently
  • Wire funds to almost anyone, almost anywhere
opens a pop up video

Watch the video to see how to send money using just an email address or mobile number.

Getting started:

  1. 1. Sign in to Online Banking and click Transfers.
  2. 1. Sign in to Mobile Banking and click Transfers.
  3. 2. Choose the transfer type you wish to make.
  4. 3. Follow the instructions to complete your transfer.
Remember: Your Online Banking transactions are protected with our Online and Mobile Banking Security Guarantee.Footnote4

Advertising Practices

We strive to provide you with information about products and services you might find interesting and useful. Relationship-based ads and online behavioral advertising help us do that.

Here's how it works: We gather information about your online activities, such as the searches you conduct on our Sites and the pages you visit. This information may be used to deliver advertising on our Sites and offline (for example, by phone, email and direct mail) that's customized to meet specific interests you may have.

If you prefer that we do not use this information, you may opt out of online behavioral advertising Opens link in a new window. If you opt out, though, you may still receive generic advertising. In addition, financial advisors/Client Managers may continue to use information collected online to provide product and service information in accordance with account agreements.

Also, if you opt out of online behavioral advertising, you may still see ads when you sign in to your account, for example through Online Banking or MyMerrill. These ads are based on your specific account relationships with us.

To learn more about relationship-based ads, online behavioral advertising and our privacy practices, please review the Bank of America Online Privacy Notice and our Online Privacy FAQs Opens link in a new window.
